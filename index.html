Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of operations research and machine learning, with a focus on sequential decision-making, learning theory, and optimization. Specifically, I study how to design and analyze algorithms for multi-agent systems, including multi-agent multi-armed bandit problems and multi-agent preference learning (such as reinforcement learning from human feedback for LLM alignment), across both online and offline settings. Ultimately, I aim to address the challenges posed by imperfect environments arising from limited or biased data, and to develop algorithms that better capture the complexities of real-world applications.
